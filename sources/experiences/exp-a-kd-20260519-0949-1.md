## Solution

Replace the single-thread serial cumsum with a parallel Blelloch prefix scan on a power-of-2 scan_buf where all threads cooperatively perform log2(scan_size)=6 up-sweep and 6 down-sweep phases, distributing the prefix computation across the entire block

## Key Lessons

- Replace serial prefix-sum with parallel Blelloch scan when element count exceeds ~16 — O(log n) parallel depth with full thread participation outperforms single-thread O(n) loop despite added __syncthreads() barriers per level
- For small scan sizes (64 elements) the counting step (atomicAdd over numel elements) still dominates kernel time, so the speedup from parallelizing the prefix scan alone is modest — focus optimization on the dominant phase first
