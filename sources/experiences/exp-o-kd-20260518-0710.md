## Key Lessons

- The 128KB allocation was unnecessarily large because the radix algorithm uses s_input_idx[][SMEM_INPUT_SIZE] as a ping-pong buffer for two rounds, and each round only needs at most ~TopK (2048) candidate entries in the threshold bin, not the full 16K entries the old SMEM_INPUT_SIZE allowed.
