## Solution

Introduce silu2_v2 that directly produces __nv_bfloat162 via make_bfloat162(__float2bfloat16_rn(...)) and fuse the SiLU activation with the element-wise multiply into a single loop, eliminating the intermediate results_bf162 array and reducing register pressure

## Key Lessons

- Fusing SiLU activation with element-wise multiply into a single loop eliminates an intermediate register array and reduces register pressure, which benefits pipelined kernels where registers are scarce — but shows negligible gain in memory-bound microbenchmarks where compute is not the bottleneck
- Prefer make_bfloat162(__float2bfloat16_rn(...)) over __float22bfloat162_rn when constructing bfloat162 from independently computed float lanes, as it gives per-element control and avoids the float2 intermediate
