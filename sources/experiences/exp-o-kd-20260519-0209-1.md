## Solution

Switch from CausalMask<false> to CausalMask<true> (IsQBegin=true) by removing the offset_q term from the max_blocks_q calculation, so each Q-block only visits K-blocks up to its own position, reducing total tile visits from 128 to 10 and eliminating 118 unnecessary inner-loop iterations and their associated global memory accesses

## Key Lessons

- Causal mask dispatch must distinguish whether the Q sequence starts at the beginning of the KV cache (IsQBegin=true) — when it does, the offset_q = N_K - N_Q term is unnecessary and causes massive over-computation by visiting K-blocks that causal masking should skip
- Dispatch logic in tiled attention kernels is a high-leverage optimization target: a single arithmetic term change in the trip-count function can eliminate the majority of tile visits when N_Q << N_K, as the speedup is proportional to the reduction in total tile iterations
