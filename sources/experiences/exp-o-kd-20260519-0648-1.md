## Solution

Replace Blelloch scan with a warp shuffle (__shfl_up_sync) based two-level exclusive prefix sum: intra-warp scan via warp_exclusive_scan (no __syncthreads needed within a warp), then warp 0 aggregates per-warp partial sums and broadcasts offsets back, reducing total __syncthreads() barriers from ~13 to 6

## Key Lessons

- Replace Blelloch scan with warp shuffle (__shfl_up_sync) based two-level exclusive prefix sum when scan_size is small (few hundred elements) — warp shuffle avoids shared-memory bank conflicts and eliminates per-iteration __syncthreads() within each warp, reducing total barriers from O(2·log n) to O(log(num_warps))
- Two-level scan strategy (intra-warp via __shfl_up_sync, inter-warp via warp 0 aggregate) reduces __syncthreads() from ~13 to 6 for scan_size=64 — applies whenever the scan spans multiple warps but fits in a single block
