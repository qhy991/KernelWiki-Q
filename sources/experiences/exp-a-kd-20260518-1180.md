## Challenge

The before kernel uses a single kv_scale (4.0) for both K and V dequantization, which produces numerically incorrect results when the correct per-tensor scales differ (k_scale=3.0, v_scale=5.0)


## Solution

The after kernel correctly applies k_scale to K and v_scale to V. However, the computational pattern is identical: both perform exactly one multiply per element for K and one for V. The two extra float parameters in the after kernel signature are pass-by-value and do not affect register pressure ...
