## Solution

Guard the warp_reduce_sum call with if(renormalize) so the entire warp cooperative reduction is skipped when renormalize=false, eliminating 5 shuffle-add operations per loop iteration and their associated pipeline stalls

## Key Lessons

- Guard expensive warp cooperative operations (shuffle-based reductions) behind the runtime condition that actually needs their result — when the result is unused, skipping the entire warp communication avoids pipeline stalls from cross-lane synchronization
- Unconditional warp shuffle reductions compound overhead across loop iterations — even though each shuffle-add is cheap, 5 operations per iteration multiplied across all threads creates measurable latency that a simple conditional guard eliminates
