KernelEvalPlus RMS Norm optimization (RN_04, fp32, LLaMA-3-8B, hidden_size=4096)

## Key Lessons

- __launch_bounds__(N, min_blocks) is a low-cost optimization that can yield 10-15% gains by reducing register spilling
- Adaptive kernel selection (different thread counts per batch size) is important for RMS norm across batch sizes
- float4 vectorized reads + warp-shuffle reduction is the correct baseline for RMS norm on hidden_size=4096
- Single-kernel approaches with aggressive unrolling can be worse than multi-variant adaptive selection
