## Challenge

In sync_per_iter_before.cu, 8 iterations with two __syncthreads() each produce 16 barriers. In sync_per_iter_after.cu, the coarsened loop with FPP=2 reduces this to 8 barriers. The 17% speedup reflects the eliminated barrier overhead


## Solution

The after kernel uses twice the SMEM (smem_stride*2) to hold two fragments simultaneously, but this is a worthwhile tradeoff because SMEM capacity on A800 (shared memory per block) is sufficient and the reduced synchronization improves throughput.
