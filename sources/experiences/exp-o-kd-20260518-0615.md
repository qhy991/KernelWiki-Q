half_d4096

## Solution

The after version's gelu_kernel computes one FMUL (f * ALPHA), then ::erf, then three final ops (0.5 * f * (1+erf)) — totaling 4 arithmetic ops plus one SFU call. The 3 fewer arithmetic instructions before the transcendental call reduce register pressure and instruction latency in the inner loop....

## Key Lessons

- The kernel is still memory-bound but less overwhelmingly so than the float case because more arithmetic per thread increases compute utilization.
