## Challenge

In the before code, R_D stores half values in 2 uint32_t registers per element. The online softmax rescale at each K/V tile step performs 4 half2float conversions (loading the FP16 accumulator), an fmaf in FP32, then 4 float2half conversions (storing back to FP16) per kWarpTileHeadDimV iteration....


## Solution
