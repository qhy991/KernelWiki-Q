## Solution

Increase bins from 512 to 2048 and replace the single all-bins BlockScan with a round-based loop (CASE_BINS/CASE_THREADS rounds) that performs one BlockScan per round and uses __syncthreads_or(foundThreshold) to break immediately once the threshold bin is located, often exiting in 1-2 rounds instead of scanning all 4

## Key Lessons

- Use round-based BlockScan with __syncthreads_or early-exit instead of a single all-bins scan when searching for a threshold bin — finer bins (2048 vs 512) place the threshold earlier in sorted order so the loop often exits in 1-2 of 4 rounds, saving significant prefix-sum work
- Carry a running lastValue across scan rounds to maintain a correct cumulative prefix-sum when breaking the scan into chunks — each round's prefix must be offset by the total count from all previous rounds
