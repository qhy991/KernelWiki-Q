- For z16, __hsub(__int2half_rn(-64), __int2half_rn(zero)) replaces the float division with an integer subtraction on the GPU.
- Applied change: Replaced float-arithmetic zero-point construction with IEEE 754 bit-level manipulation: half_uint16(0xe400 | zero) constructs the half representation of (-1024 - zero) by OR-ing the runtime zero value directly into the known bit pattern for -1024.0f (0xe400 in half-precision).
