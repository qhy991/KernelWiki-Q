## Challenge

Trying BLOCK_SIZE=256 TILE_SIZE=256 V_CHUNK=128 for better SM occupancy

## Key Lessons

- Reducing BLOCK_SIZE from 512 to 256 with TILE_SIZE=256 hurt performance (ratio 267.9->209.4) despite better theoretical occupancy
- Smaller TILE_SIZE means more tiles to process and less parallelism in QK phase, hurting throughput
- For flash attention with seq_len=8192, larger TILE_SIZE=512 with BLOCK_SIZE=512 is better than smaller tiles with smaller blocks
- The v3 configuration (BLOCK_SIZE=512, TILE_SIZE=512, V_CHUNK=128) remains the best at ratio=267.9
