hgemm_1024

## Solution

v_ceil(1024,128)=8 to (8+1-1)/1=8, which is the same. The regression likely comes from the bx computation itself: bx=((int)BLOCK_SWIZZLE)*blockIdx.z*gridDim.x+blockIdx.x adds blockIdx.z*gridDim.x=0*8=0 at z=0, so bx=blockIdx.x. But the 3D grid shape may affect the GPU's block scheduling differently 

## Key Lessons

- The no-swizzle 2D grid (dim3(8,8)) allows the hardware to schedule blocks in a naturally cache-friendly order, while the 3D grid (dim3(8,8,1)) may disrupt this scheduling.
