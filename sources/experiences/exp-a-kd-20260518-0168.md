## Solution

Replace FastDivmod with hardcoded integer div/mod by 2, and narrow remapping to only the first two waves (2*avail_sms blocks) when sm_occupancy > 1 and grid has at least 4 waves, eliminating struct overhead and avoiding unnecessary index shuffling for later waves

## Key Lessons

- Replacing FastDivmod (float-multiply-based division) with hardcoded integer div/mod by a compile-time constant eliminates struct register pressure and float-multiply overhead when the divisor is small and known at compile time
- Narrowing block remapping scope to only the first two waves avoids unnecessary index shuffling for later waves that naturally spread across SMs, reducing overhead without sacrificing SM utilization for early-wave latency hiding
