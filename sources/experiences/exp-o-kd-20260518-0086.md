fused_row_reduction

## Solution

The after source keeps the reduction inside one block-local kernel and reuses the shared-memory reduction result immediately, so it removes the extra round trip and the additional launch overhead.

## Key Lessons

- The before pair splits the row reduction and the consumer into two kernels, which forces a full write of the per-row `di` buffer to global memory and a second launch to read it back.
