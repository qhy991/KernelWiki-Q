multihead_hd128_512t

## Solution

The optimization is designed for SM 9.0 (H100) where TMA/cp.async throughput is higher and the occupancy trade-offs differ. On SM 8.0 the benefit is marginal.

## Key Lessons

- On A800 (SM 8.0), the 4% improvement does not cross the 5% threshold because: (1) the before kernel already has very high grid-level occupancy at 512*64=32768 warps, fully utilizing the GPU; (2) the after kernel reduces the grid to 512*16=8192 warps, which may underutilize the GPU at this batch size
