## Solution

Replace the serial head loop with a head-parallel kernel where blockIdx.y selects the head index (0..num_qo_heads-1 for Q, num_qo_heads..num_qo_heads+num_kv_heads-1 for K), distributing 72 heads across 72 independent thread blocks; add occupancy-based adaptive dispatch that queries cudaOccupancyMaxActiveBlocksPerMultiprocessor to choose head-parallel mode when token blocks are insufficient to saturate SMs

## Key Lessons

- Replace per-block head loops with blockIdx.y head mapping to distribute independent head computations across separate SMs — applies whenever num_heads >> batch_size and head processing is independent
- Use cudaOccupancyMaxActiveBlocksPerMultiprocessor + cudaDevAttrMultiProcessorCount to compute total available CTAs and compare against required token-level blocks to decide whether head-parallel dispatch is beneficial — avoids the overhead of head-parallel launch when token-level parallelism already saturates the GPU
- Pack Q and K heads into a single blockIdx.y dimension (Q heads first, K heads offset by num_qo_heads) to launch exactly (num_qo_heads + num_kv_heads) blocks without separate kernel dispatches for Q and K
