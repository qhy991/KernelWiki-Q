## Key Lessons

- The speedup is larger for moe_block_size=32 than for moe_block_size=16 because the serial scan cost scales linearly with moe_block_size while the after code's cost is nearly constant.
- The key difference is that the before code's serial scan latency grows proportionally with block size (8 sequential global loads vs 4), while the after code's latency stays near-constant because the shared-memory load is parallelized and the warp reduction is always 5 stages regardless of block size
