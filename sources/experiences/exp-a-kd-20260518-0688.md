## Challenge

The before approach uses two separate kernels: compute_scores_with_bias_kernel writes sigmoid(scores)+bias to an intermediate scores_with_bias buffer via a full global-memory pass, then topk_with_k2_before_kernel reads from that buffer in the <=WARP_SIZE branch (largest=input[i])


## Solution

The after approach fuses sigmoid_accurate() and bias addition directly into the top2 loop: value=sigmoid_accurate(value); value=value+group_bias[i]; largest=value.
