## Solution

Replace FastDivmod with hardcoded integer division/modulo by 2, limiting remapping to only the first two waves (2*avail_sms blocks) where SM spreading matters most, and guarding with a minimum grid-size check (4*avail_sms) to skip remapping when the grid is too small to benefit

## Key Lessons

- Hardcoded integer div/mod (e.g., block_idx / 2, block_idx % 2) compiles to a single IMAD or bit-shift instruction, eliminating the float multiply + correction overhead of FastDivmod — applies whenever the divisor is a known small constant
- Block-to-SM remapping only benefits the first two scheduling waves; later waves are naturally spread by the hardware scheduler, so remapping beyond 2*avail_sms blocks is wasted computation
