## Challenge

For Float8_ (4 float2 pairs), the naive approach computes 4 independent muls producing 4 float2 intermediates (p0-p3), then tree-adds them: add(p0,p1)||add(p2,p3) -> add(t0,t1). This requires 4 float2 registers for intermediates (8 FP32 registers). The FMA approach chains: mul(a.x,b.x) -> fma(a.y,b.
