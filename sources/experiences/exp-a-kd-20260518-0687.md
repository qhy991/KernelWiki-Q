## Key Lessons

- In this isolated microbenchmark, the stride-based before kernel is faster because nvcc -O3 optimizes the constant stride multiplications (512, 16, 4, 1) to equivalent shift/AND operations and hoists the per-thread numKTiles computation out of the inner loop.
- The __forceinline__ + precomputed numKTiles + compact shift-OR formula eliminated that overhead and reduced register pressure in the calling context, producing a net gain that this fully-inlined isolated arithmetic microbenchmark cannot reproduce.
