fp16_32h_128d

## Solution

the optimization is zero-cost when disabled. The measured ~2.1x speedup closely matches the theoretical 2x from halving write volume, with slight overhead from the conversion instruction.

## Key Lessons

- The after kernel writes uint8_t (1-byte FP8-E5M2) elements to cache instead of half_t (2-byte FP16) in the before kernel.
