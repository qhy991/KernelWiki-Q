fp32_top_k_sampling_qwen2.5-7b_k8_ne0256

## Solution

Per-thread top-k selection with insertion sort + curand-based categorical sampling

## Key Lessons

- Sampling operations require fixed seed for reproducible testing
- For small K (<=8), simple insertion sort is efficient enough
- curand_uniform with cumulative sum works well for categorical sampling
- Per-thread approach works well for batch_size up to 512
