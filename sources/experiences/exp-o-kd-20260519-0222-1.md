## Solution

Replace six plain integer div/mod operations with FastDivmod::divmod() calls that compile to __umulhi (single-cycle on SM80) plus a shift and multiply-subtract, eliminating ~120 cycles of integer division latency per activation access

## Key Lessons

- Replace hot-loop integer division/modulo with FastDivmod (magic-number division via __umulhi) to reduce each div/mod from ~20 cycles to ~1 cycle — applies whenever loop-invariant divisors are used for index decomposition
- Precompute FastDivmod objects from loop-invariant divisors outside the loop; the magic multiplier and shift are constant across all iterations, so the setup cost is amortized to zero
