## Solution

Pre-compute the reciprocal of the scale once at kernel entry (1.0f / *output_scale) and replace all per-element divisions with multiplications (val * scale_inv), exploiting the fact that GPU FP32 multiplication has ~4x higher throughput than division

## Key Lessons

- Replace per-element division with pre-computed reciprocal multiplication when the divisor is uniform across elements — GPU FP32 multiplication throughput is ~4x higher than division, and a single reciprocal computation amortizes over thousands of elements
- The benefit of instruction-level optimizations (div→mul) diminishes at smaller problem sizes where memory latency and kernel launch overhead dominate total execution time — the same optimization may show larger speedups at larger tensor sizes
