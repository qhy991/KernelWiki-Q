half_output

## Solution

plicit float→half conversion. On SM80+ with nvcc -O3, both paths generate equivalent PTX: the explicit __float2half() and the implicit conversion both produce a F2F.F64.F32 or equivalent h

## Key Lessons

- Since the computation is dominated by the float arithmetic (multiplication, sign selection, table lookup), the conversion method makes no measurable difference.
