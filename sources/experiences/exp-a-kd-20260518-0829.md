float_sparse

## Solution

_xor_sync already guarantees that all 32 lanes have both contributed and received their shuffled values before any thread can proceed past the call. However, in this compact 256-block kernel on A800 (sm_80), the two extra barrier instructions are absorbed into the same warp instruction pipeline slot

## Key Lessons

- The second __syncwarp() is also redundant because __shfl_xor_sync already guarantees that all 32 lanes have both contributed and received their shuffled values before any thread can proceed past the call.
- The nvcc -O3 compiler may also recognize the no-op nature of these syncwarps in this context and optimize their scheduling overlap.
