## Solution

Fuse global_scale into the topk weight at load time by pre-multiplying global_scale_val with the converted half2 topk weight, reducing the per-element hot path from two __hmul2 calls to one when the fused weight is reused across iterations

## Key Lessons

- Fusing a constant scale factor into a per-element weight at load time reduces per-output-element multiplies from two to one — but the savings only materialize when the fused weight is reused across multiple output writes (e.g., across K iterations in a matmul loop), not in single-write-per-element microbenchmarks
- Microbenchmarks with one output write per element may show neutral results for operation fusion optimizations that require a broader computational context (full matmul pipeline) to demonstrate the reuse benefit
