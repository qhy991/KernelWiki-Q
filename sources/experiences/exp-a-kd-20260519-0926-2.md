## Solution

Add #pragma unroll directive and replace WARP_SIZE/2 with compile-time constant 16 in the shuffle reduction loop, enabling the compiler to emit a fixed sequence of 5 SHFL instructions with zero loop control overhead

## Key Lessons

- Apply #pragma unroll with compile-time constant bounds on warp shuffle reduction loops → generates fixed SHFL instruction sequences without loop counter and branch overhead → applies to all warp-level reduction patterns (sum, max, argmax) with known iteration counts
