## Solution

Replace occupancy-rounded waveset ceiling division with fast_max(work_blocks, avail_sms*4), launching only the 600 blocks that do real work while guaranteeing at least avail_sms*4 blocks for sufficient GPU occupancy

## Key Lessons

- Rounding grid size to gpu_occupancy multiples (sm_occupancy × avail_sms) can massively over-allocate blocks for multi-occupancy kernels — use fast_max(work_blocks, avail_sms*4) to launch only real work while maintaining sufficient occupancy
- Idle CTAs from over-allocated grids still incur scheduler overhead and launch cost; eliminating them improves latency even when GPU utilization is already high
- The ceiling-division waveset formula (work + occ - 1) / occ × occ is appropriate for single-occupancy kernels but breaks down when sm_occupancy > 1 inflates the rounding granularity
