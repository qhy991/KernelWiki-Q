## Solution

Use the swap_ab identity C^T = B^T * A^T (CUBLAS_OP_T on both operands) so that N=4096 becomes the effective leading M dimension in cuBLAS, making it large and aligned for tensor cores while eliminating all padding, zero-fill, and output slicing operations

## Key Lessons

- When M is small and non-aligned but N or K is large, use the GEMM transpose identity C^T = B^T * A^T to swap the small dimension into the K or N position, avoiding padding overhead entirely — applies whenever one GEMM dimension is misaligned but another is large enough to dominate tile utilization
- Padding a small M dimension for cuBLAS incurs cudaMemset + cudaMemcpy2D (input) + cudaMemcpy2D (output) overhead that can dominate total latency for small-batch GEMMs — a single transposed call eliminates all three auxiliary operations
- cuBLAS treats the leading dimension independently from the operation transpose flags, so CUBLAS_OP_T on both operands with swapped pointer/lda arguments is mathematically equivalent to the original C = A * B without any data transformation
