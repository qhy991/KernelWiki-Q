## Solution

Replace six plain integer div/mod operations with three FastDivmod objects that use __umulhi (single multiply-high instruction) plus shift to compute quotient/remainder, reducing each division from ~20 cycles to ~3 cycles on SM80

## Key Lessons

- Use FastDivmod (__umulhi + shift) instead of plain integer div/mod for hot-loop index decomposition — the multiply-high instruction is ~6x faster than the integer divide path on SM80 and the technique applies whenever the divisor is loop-invariant
- Precompute FastDivmod objects outside the loop when divisors (ZPQ, PQ, Q) are constant across iterations — the one-time setup cost is amortized over millions of accesses
