## Key Lessons

- The ~0.12% latency difference is within measurement noise because the dominant cost is the inner loop over 128 blocks * 16 tokens per block accumulating logit*v products, which has identical workload regardless of stride value.
- The stride arithmetic (multiplying by 8192 vs 1024) compiles to the same IMAD instruction pattern on GPU.
