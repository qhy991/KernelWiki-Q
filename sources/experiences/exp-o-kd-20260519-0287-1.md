## Solution

Replace the occupancy-rounded waveset formula with a two-tier approach: return the exact count for small grids (work_blocks <= avail_sms*2), otherwise return fast_max(work_blocks, avail_sms*4) to ensure at least 4 waves of CTAs for GPU saturation while avoiding the massive over-allocation from waveset rounding

## Key Lessons

- Ceiling-division waveset grid sizing (rounding up to multiples of sm_occupancy * avail_sms) over-allocates by up to sm_occupancy-1 extra waves for multi-occupancy kernels — prefer fast_max with a fixed minimum wave count to bound over-allocation
- A two-tier grid sizing strategy (exact count for small grids, fast_max for large) avoids both under-utilization from returning too few blocks and over-allocation from aggressive waveset rounding — applies to any kernel launch helper where sm_occupancy > 1
