## Challenge

With 256 experts filling exactly one 256-thread block, all threads perform work. ## Solution

The optimization's value is in routing quality, not filter speed: problems with moderate M but high AI now bypass the swap path, and problems with large M but low AI now enter the swap path, improving downstream GEMM throughput. A filter-kernel microbenchmark cannot capture this downstream benefit.
