## Solution

The fix switches the alignment predicate to stride(2), the per-head stride in the [batch, seqlen, heads, dim] layout. That stride reflects the spacing between adjacent heads in memory and therefore matches the alignment requirement of the packed query/key/value accesses used by the aligned kernel...
