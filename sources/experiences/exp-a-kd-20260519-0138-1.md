## Solution

Replace the triple-nested unrolled loop with conditional branch by direct O(1) integer division/modulo arithmetic on ldsm_vec_num to compute the three indices (access_m_idx, inner_idx, inst_m_idx), eliminating loop overhead and producing fewer surviving constructor instructions after nvcc optimization

## Key Lessons

- Replace unrolled loops with conditional branches by direct integer div/mod arithmetic when the loop index maps linearly to the search value — even when nvcc can theoretically constant-fold, the direct O(1) computation produces fewer surviving instructions in hot constructor paths
- Parameterizing compile-time constants (InstructionShape as a template parameter instead of a hardcoded typedef) makes the refactoring to direct arithmetic natural and enables the compiler to validate constraints via static_assert
