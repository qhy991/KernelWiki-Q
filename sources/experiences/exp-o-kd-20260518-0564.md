## Challenge

In the tall_skinny case, the before source (tall_skinny_before.cu) evaluates k=4096 > 3*n=768 and enters the split-K StreamK branch. This branch calls row_major_sgemm four times with k_per_split=1024, writing partial results into separate dP buffers, then launches a split_k_accum reduction kernel...


## Solution

The after source (tall_skinny_after.cu) unconditionally calls row_major_sgemm once with the full K=4096 dimension on a dedicated CUDA stream. The measured 11.9% speedup comes from eliminating three extra cuBLAS kernel launches, the 4x partial-result buffer allocation, and the separate reduction k...

- **speedup**: 1.119x (improved)

- This branch calls row_major_sgemm four times with k_per_split=1024, writing partial results into separate dP buffers, then launches a split_k_accum reduction kernel to sum the partials into dC.
