## Challenge

Optimization focus: Changed schedule dispatch condition from pure K-dimension threshold to combined SM-count and K-dimension check, so Cooperative schedule is always used on H100/H200/H800 (132 SMs) while Pingpong is only used on H20 (78 SMs) with K > 128.

## Solution

PUs which have 132 SMs and more shared memory — the Pingpong schedule with its smaller tiles and 2-SM-per-tile pairing underutilizes the abundant SMs on these high-SM-count GPUs, especially for MoE workloads where the total M dimension across experts can be large.
