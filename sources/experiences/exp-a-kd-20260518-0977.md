## Key Lessons

- In this isolated microbenchmark, both approaches achieve nearly identical throughput (0.964x, delta=3.75%) because the nvcc compiler heavily optimizes the naive path: it recognizes the shift/mask/subtract pattern and maps it to efficient PTX.
- The LOP3-based dequantization in lop3_dequant_after.cu replaces shift/mask/OR with two lop3.b32 instructions that fuse nibble extraction and fp16 exponent injection, then applies __hsub2 and __hfma2 with magic constants.
