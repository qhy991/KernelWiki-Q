## Solution

Skip the beta*source multiply entirely under NoBetaScaling by directly assigning source_frag to intermediate, eliminating one element-wise loop (8 FFMA instructions per thread) from the epilogue path

## Key Lessons

- Eliminating redundant multiplies in GEMM epilogue when beta=1.0 (NoBetaScaling) is arithmetically correct but may not produce measurable speedup at small workload sizes where kernel launch overhead and memory traffic dominate — the optimization is most beneficial when epilogue arithmetic is a throughput bottleneck on large tensors
- Compilers at -O3 may partially optimize identity multiplies (beta=1.0) into register copies, further eroding any observable arithmetic throughput difference in microbenchmarks
