## Challenge

The before kernel performs the prefix sum serially on a single thread (threadIdx.x == 0) iterating over all 64 experts with warp-based index decomposition (div/mod) into shared_counts, while 1023 other threads wait at a __syncthreads() barrier


## Solution

The after kernel replaces this with a Blelloch parallel prefix scan where all threads participate in log2(64)=6 up-sweep and 6 down-sweep phases, each with a __syncthreads(). - The direct expert_id indexing (atomicAdd(&shared_counts[expert_id]) instead of warp-based div/mod decomposition) and the cooperative binary-search expert_ids fill also contribute small gains by reducing integer arithmetic overhead and distributing work across more threads.
