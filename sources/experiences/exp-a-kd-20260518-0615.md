## Key Lessons

- The 1.477x speedup is slightly lower than the 1.579x of the smaller case because at larger scale the kernel launch overhead becomes a smaller fraction of total execution time, so the dominant savings come from halving the global memory traffic rather than eliminating launch overhead.
- Same fusion pattern as the smaller case but with 8192 rows and topk=8, producing a larger output array (65536 float elements).
