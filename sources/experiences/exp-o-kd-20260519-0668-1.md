## Solution

Fuse the alignment and padding kernels into a single fused_align_and_padding_kernel launch — after the alignment phase completes (threadIdx.x==0 writes total_tokens_post_pad, then __syncthreads()), all threads cooperatively perform the padding fill within the same kernel, eliminating one full kernel submission cycle and its associated parameter setup overhead

## Key Lessons

- Kernel fusion eliminates per-launch submission overhead (~2–3 μs each) — most impactful when individual kernel execution times are short (<20 μs), as launch overhead becomes a dominant fraction of total wall-clock time
