## Solution

Remove the offset_q offset term by dispatching with IsQBegin=true (CausalMask<true>), so max_blocks_q = ceil_div((blk+1)*tile_q, tile_k) produces the correct triangular trip count (blk+1), eliminating 118 unnecessary tile visits across 4 Q-blocks

## Key Lessons

- Causal mask dispatch must distinguish whether the Q sequence starts at the beginning of the KV sequence (IsQBegin=true) — when true, no offset correction is needed and the trip count naturally forms a triangle (blk+1 blocks per row), avoiding massive over-computation
- An incorrect offset in the causal trip count formula can inflate loop iterations by an order of magnitude (128 vs 10 trips here) because every Q-block redundantly processes nearly all K-blocks instead of only the causally allowed subset
